Problem Definition 

Students are busy. A big contributor for students not doing work is because of the friction it takes to start and not knowing what to do. Students need a study planner that tells them what to do, is easy to use, and is able to adapt to a busy student’s changing schedule. 

 

Functional Requirements 

The software will act as a time planner/calendar for students, as they can be very busy, it is easier for them to follow a premade guide than to plan their time. The process should be as automated as possible, with students using an AI to input calendar events rather than manual additions. Students will have the ability to add homework, revision and study material. Schoolwork is then inserted into the students calendar, they will have the option to choose when the event is completed or to let the AI decide based on when the student is free, what is most important, and how long each task will take to complete. Adding schoolwork should be as easy as possible, as minimal friction will make the student more likely to use and follow the planner, for this to be the case. Students will need to be able to mark off tasks they have completed, and the AI will be able to reschedule the calendar when a task is not completed or goes overtime. Users will need an account. 

Non-Functional Requirements  

The program needs to be secure, with strong authentication, authorisation and good availability. Data should be saved securely and safely. UI/UX should be very clean, modern and usable.
